Data Analysis and Reporting Website for Lettuce Growing Oxygenation IOT Experiment
  • Github
  • Site Preview
This project was to text the efficacy of different plant water resivoir oxygen diffusion methods. We had four tanks with different aeration methods ranging from no aeration, to LTF stone, to standard air stone, and micro bubble pump. We grew the lettuce for four months and at the end up the process compared the yields in the different rows by aeration method. We were able to quantitatively rank the methods given the lettuce yields. We tracked the water pH, electrical conductivity, dissolved oxygen and temperature as well as some environmental variables in the greenhouse.
The technology for this was built on Mysql, Javascript, D3.js, Bootstrap, and HTML/CSS.
Intelligent Webpage Built for a University Professor
  • Github
  • Site Preview
A professor whom I worked with approached me to redesign his professor profile site. This is a particularly prolific professor with over 100 publications. He was having issues keeping his page up to date. I worked with him to figure out what his requirements were and designed a webpage to be intelligent for him. The site reads all of the data for the listings from a JSON data repository and the professor now only needs to fill in a new entry each time he wants to update his site. The site automaically reorders the data and classifies it by date and other factors.
The technology for this was built on HTML/CSS, Javascript, Bootstrap and Jquery
