This project was created to monitor the conditions in an outdoor hoophouse and assess the efficacy of differing methods of resovior aeration. The project was build on the Arduino Yun platform. We used some custom developed 3d printed parts as well as some custom sensor design. We used ds18b20 temperature sensors, atlas scientific pH, EC and DO sensors as well as ambient light and humidity sensors. The hardware was assembled in a standard NEMA box with all connections soldered. We were monitoring four channels of lettuce for several months. We found ultimately that the differing methods of aeration achieved differing levels of lettuce production as well as changing the chemical makeup of the water due to differing levels of bacteria production and acticity.
